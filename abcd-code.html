Image & Personal Brand

1. THE ABCD CODE (B2B + B2C)

Audience: For Individuals / Groups / Students (B2C) | Corporate Workshops & Schools (B2B)

Description: Image & Personal Brand Reinvention

Duration: 8 weeks / 16 Classes (Customizable) | 1 Hour per module (B2B)

CTA: Explore This Program / Connect with an Inner Leap Consultant

Modules + Focus Area

  • Image Styling & Appearance - Style strategy, formality systems, personal style, visual identity, grooming
  • Body Language - Posture, gestures, movement-based expression
  • Communication - Vocal tone, clarity, assertiveness
  • Digital Presence - LinkedIn, online persona, content visibility (coming soon)
  • Show & Tell - Full presentation + Integrated presence (Brand Journey showcase)

Outcome: Participants gain mastery over visual identity, posture, voice, and digital presence. They learn to decode formality levels, align their style to content, command attention through body language, and project credibility, both online and offline, building a consistent, confident personal brand.
