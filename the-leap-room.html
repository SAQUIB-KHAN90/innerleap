THE LEAP ROOM (SIGNATURE PROGRAM)

Audience: For Individuals / Groups / Students (B2C) | Corporate & Education Industry (B2B)

Description: Transforming Through Performing Arts + Voice + Speech & Presentation

Modules + Focus Area:

    The Body Speaks – Basics of posture, breath, bhaav, body mapping

    Dance & Confidence – Fusion choreography + confidence building

    The Voice Within – Vocal tone, modulation, and emotional range

    Script Yourself – Skit on personal journey + emotion through drama + exchanging ideas and creating partnerships

    Find Your Voice – Musical therapy + modulation + Transaction Analysis (Ego State shift)

    Stage-Ready – Stage delivery preparation

Outcome: Participants reconnect with their bodies, voices, and stories to build authentic confidence and stage presence. Whether it's reclaiming self-worth or elevating leadership, The Leap Room rewires not just how you perform but who you shape up to be. It also helps you rediscover or shift your ego state with the most effective co-learning when you 'FIND YOUR VOICE'.
