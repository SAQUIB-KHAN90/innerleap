Disruptive Manager (B2B + B2C)

Audience: For Individuals / Groups (B2C) | Corporate Workshop (B2B)

Description: Leadership Upskilling for First-Time Managers

Duration: 8 weeks / 16 Classes | 8 Hours Training Package (B2B)

Modules + Focus Area:

    You're not the Boss yet! - Mindset reset & feedback

    Culture & Chaos - Safe teams + DEI

    Coaching Conversations - Better listening & coaching

    EQ + Decision Design - Emotion awareness

    Futureproof Yourself - Curiosity hacks & career prep

Outcome: First-time managers shift from doing to leading. They build emotional agility, learn to coach, handle feedback with maturity, and foster inclusive, resilient teams, ready to lead with clarity, not just authority.
