FAQs

+ What is Inner Leap™? Is this therapy or coaching or something else?

Inner Leap™ is a unique blend of therapeutic and coaching approaches, designed to help individuals achieve personal growth and transformation. It integrates elements from various disciplines to create a holistic and personalized experience. It's not strictly therapy or coaching, but rather a synergistic approach that leverages the best of both to support your journey.

+ I'm not an artist or dancer or creative in any way. Can I still join?

Absolutely! Inner Leap™ is designed for everyone, regardless of their artistic or creative background. Our programs utilize creative expression as a tool for self-discovery and personal development, but no prior experience or talent in art, dance, or any creative field is required. The focus is on the process of exploration and growth, not on producing artistic masterpieces.

+ What kind of results can I expect from Inner Leap™?

Participants in Inner Leap™ often report a range of positive outcomes, including increased self-awareness, enhanced emotional regulation, improved relationships, greater clarity on personal goals, and a stronger sense of purpose. While individual results may vary, our programs are designed to empower you with tools and insights for lasting personal transformation and well-being.

+ Is this only for people who are going through grief or trauma?

No, Inner Leap™ is not exclusively for individuals experiencing grief or trauma. While our programs can be incredibly beneficial for those navigating challenging life circumstances, they are also designed for anyone seeking personal growth, self-discovery, and a deeper understanding of themselves. Our approach is adaptable to various needs and goals, from healing past wounds to unlocking new potential.

+ What constitutes a typical session or program?

A typical Inner Leap™ session or program is highly personalized, blending various modalities such as guided reflection, creative exercises, movement, mindfulness, and facilitated discussions. Sessions are designed to be interactive and experiential, fostering a safe and supportive environment for exploration. The exact structure and duration of a program depend on your individual needs and the specific program you choose.

+ Who will I be collaborating with? Are the facilitators trained?

You will be collaborating with highly trained and experienced facilitators who are experts in their respective fields. Our facilitators undergo rigorous training in various therapeutic and coaching methodologies, ensuring they are equipped to guide you through your Inner Leap™ journey with professionalism, empathy, and expertise. We prioritize creating a supportive and collaborative environment for all participants.
