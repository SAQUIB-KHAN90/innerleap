Ankit Sharma – Co-Founder

Ankit Sharma brings nearly two decades of high-impact customer operations leadership to the heart of people transformation at Inner Leap™. His expertise lies at the intersection of performance strategy, human behavior, and customer-first cultures shaping environments where operational excellence meets inner evolution.

Across his career, Ankit has led global support teams, reimagined service delivery models, and turned low-performing CSAT scores into enduring trust-based success stories. But beyond the dashboards and data lies a deeper truth he has come to embody: people rarely underperform due to lack of process; they underperform due to lack of clarity, courage, and connection.

At Inner Leap™, Ankit combines operational rigour with a deeply human lens. He architects learning ecosystems and coaching models that are grounded in both capability and self-belief. From performance transformation frameworks to emotionally intelligent CX innovation strategies, his work equips teams and leaders to leap to their next level, not through fixing, but through re-purposing.

Whether guiding a CXO through a high-stakes escalation, enabling frontline leaders to own their performance journeys, or breaking patterns of burnout through customer sentiment insights, Ankit turns metrics into momentum. With Inner Leap™ as your trusted advisor, success is redefined-measurable, scalable, and sustainable.

With Ankit at the helm of performance transformation, systems are not just functional, they are deeply human. Transformation he believes, isn't a switch. It's a strategy and he is here to architect both.
