PERFORMING ARTS & EXPRESSIVE THERAPY

1. THE ART OF POSSIBILITY

Author: Rosamund Stone Zander & Benjamin Zander

Overview: This book offers a way of breakthrough, a roadmap for creativity in all human endeavors.

2. EXPRESSIVE ARTS THERAPY

Author: Cathy A. Malchiodi

Overview: A comprehensive guide exploring the use of expressive arts in therapy, emphasizing the healing power of creative expression.

EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE & SELF-MASTERY

1. EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

Author: Travis Bradberry & Jean Greaves

Overview: A practical guide to improving emotional intelligence skills and self-awareness.

LEADERSHIP & PERSONAL BRANDING
