PERFORMING ARTS & EXPRESSIVE THERAPY

1. THE ART OF POSSIBILITY

Author: Rosamund Stone Zander & Benjamin Zander

Overview: This book offers a way of breakthrough, a roadmap for creativity in all human endeavors.

2. EXPRESSIVE ARTS THERAPY

Author: Cathy A. Malchiodi

Overview: A comprehensive guide exploring the use of expressive arts in therapy, emphasizing the healing power of creative expression.

EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE & SELF-MASTERY

1. EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

Author: Travis Bradberry & Jean Greaves

Overview: A practical guide to improving emotional intelligence skills and self-awareness.

2. DANIEL GOLEMAN OMNIBUS
(EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE, WORKING WITH EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE & SOCIAL INTELLIGENCE: Why it matters more than IQ)

Author: Daniel Goleman

Overview: A compilation of works on emotional and social intelligence, its impact on personal and professional success.

LEADERSHIP & PERSONAL BRANDING

1. THE BRAND YOU 50

Author: Tom Peters

Overview: Focuses on personal branding, encouraging individuals to stand out and deliver exceptional value.

2. LEADERS EAT LAST: WHY SOME TEAMS PULL TOGETHER AND OTHERS DON'T

Author: Simon Sinek

Overview: Explores how leaders can create environments where people feel safe, leading to true and lasting cooperation.

Author: Elisabeth Kubler-Ross & David Kessler

Overview: A profound exploration of the five stages of grief, offering insights into the process of healing.

Overview: A compilation of innovative approaches to grief counseling, providing practical tools for therapists.
