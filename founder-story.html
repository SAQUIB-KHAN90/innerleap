FOUNDER STORY

Home / Founder Story

Inner Leap™ was founded by Rakhee Mahata, a transformation specialist, image and beauty coach, HR & industry leader, analyst, &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; training expert with over 25 years of cross-industry experience in Human Resources, Learning & Development, Talent Transformation, and Capacity Building.

Rakhee's journey began with her founding of 'Pragnya: Pragnya HR &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; Image Consulting' & 'Parakh HR', a boutique consulting outfit that rapidly gained recognition for its deep human approach, innovative and industry-strength alignments, and local leadership. Over the years, she has impacted organizations, teams, families, SMEs, schools, NGOs, IT, and education ecosystems, helping individuals and organizations learn, grow, and meaningfully grow.

Now, Rakhee's vision has taken a leap in systemic and strategic HR, Image, &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; Business transformation domain. A strong proponent that transformation is an inside job, a perpetual process. It's a behavioral experience, and a journey, personal. Her's an approach to leadership coaching, self-mastery, Motivational Interviewing, Storytelling, Image &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; Personal brand and self-styling. Implementing it through an inter-weaving of trainings, around several wellness &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; life-skills, career, personality &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; leadership, and HR developments for growth.

Rakhee's programs have made a profound impact on over 300+ in her CEO/leadership, discovering educators, HR managers, IT professionals, homemakers, artists, young adults, and entrepreneurs; helping them achieve astonishing success, and navigate transitions with grace and purpose.

Inner Leap™ represents the new chapter in this mission - a platform where one can embark on a most awaited healing, life-coaching, career transformation through an artistic, analytical, &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; purposeful journey. A one-on-one, think-tank, and hands-on strategy; one-on-one journey to discover, create, &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; accomplish your goals to live a life, where you can make impactful, &amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp; meaningful, and purposeful leap.
