FOUNDER STORY

Home / Founder Story

Inner Leap™ is helmed by Sohini Moitra, a transformation specialist, image and identity coach, HR industry leader, and performing artist with over 25 years of cross-industry experience in Human Resources, Learning & Development, Talent Transformation, and Culture Building.

Her journey began with the founding of Magic Pumpkin HR & Image Consulting LLP in 2015 , a boutique consulting studio that rapidly gained recognition for its deep human approach, rooted in authenticity, strategic alignment, and soul-led leadership. Over the years, she has led impactful interventions across startups, MSMEs, schools, BFSI, IT, and education ecosystems, helping individuals and organizations leap toward meaningful growth.

But Sohini's evolution didn't stop at systems and strategy. A lifelong practitioner of Indian classical music , dance and theatre, she recognized early on that transformation is not just a cognitive process ; it's embodied, expressive, and deeply personal. Her signature approach blends: Coaching with creativity , Behavioral Science with storytelling , Image and presence work with deep emotional release through art - Now, emerging offerings around animal welfare therapy, pet loss grief coaching, and art-based EQ development for children

Sohini's programs have made a profound difference not just at the CXO level, but also among educators, HR managers, IT professionals, homemakers, artists, young adults, and trauma survivors helping them rebuild identity, reclaim voice, and navigate transitions with grace and purpose.

Inner Leap™ represents the next chapter in this mission ; a platform where structured methods meet soulful healing. Here, transformation is lived through an artistic and personalized journey.
